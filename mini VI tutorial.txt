
****************** My mini vi tutorial ******************
********************************************************

	1. th start vi:
		$vi
		OR
		$vi <fileName>
	2. the second step: 
		press 'i' to enter the insert mode
	3. write the text you want
	4. to save:
		a. Press 'ESC'
		b. write ':w' DONT forget the ':' before the w
		OR
		:w fileNameToBeSavedin
	5. to quit vi:
		'ESC' to enter the command mode
		':q' to quit
		':q!' to quit without saving changes
